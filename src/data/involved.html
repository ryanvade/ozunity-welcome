Thank You for Your Support

Oz Unity is created for the sole purpose of helping the community and would not exist if the community did not support the project. The Oz Unity developers are very thankful for the support and we hope this distribution is as useful and fun to use as it was to make.

There are plenty of ways in which you can help the project:

Share your experience

Share with your friends and family

If you like Oz Unity, tell everyone you know. Help spread the word about not just this distribution, but others such as Manjaro and Ultimate Editon

Join the Forums and Facebook Group

Everyone is welcome to join the community. Just head to the forum and create an account or join the Facebook group. We hope to see you there!

Spread your knowledge to help others

If you are an experience Linux user, consider helping other users on the forums and Facebook group.

Project contributions

Filling Bug Reports

Bug reports let us know if software it not working correctly so that we can fix it. These bug reports can be filled on the forums.

Writers of Code

Oz Unity is entering new territory and we need help. If you know how to code (C++/C, Java, Python, HTML, and the like) and you want to contribute to the project, let us know on the forums!! We really appreciate the help!!!
